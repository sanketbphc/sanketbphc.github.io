About Me

I am Dr. Sanket Mishra, an Assistant Professor at VIT-AP University and a dedicated researcher with expertise in machine learning applications for IoT. My academic journey includes a PhD in Engineering from BITS Pilani, focusing on statistical machine translation, reinforcement learning, and sequential anomaly detection for IoT data streams.


In my role at VIT, I guide students in advanced topics like complex event processing and probabilistic modeling, and my current work explores how deep learning can optimize real-time IoT-based sensing solutions.
