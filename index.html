Biography

I aspire to enhance my proficiency in data analytics by capitalizing on the experience gained from my research in large-scale data analysis and the application of cognitive methodologies. My objective is to progress within a research-oriented institution committed to addressing real-world challenges.

Currently serving as an Associate Professor at Manipal Institute of Technology (MAHE), my research interests include complex event processing, intrusion detection systems, intelligent transportation systems, and adaptive machine learning. My work has been published in reputed journals and conferences, and I am passionate about mentoring students to excel in cutting-edge research.
